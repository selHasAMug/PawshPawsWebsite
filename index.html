Our Mission

We strive to craft the highest quality, eco-friendly paw balm that cleanses and moisturizes pet paws. With energizing warmth, we aim to make a bigger impact on pet health and bring communities together through our charity.

- Our Product -

Pawsh Pet Paw Balm

Our company is set out to create a product to moisturize the paws of animals and preserve the good health of pets.
Our balm is handmade and student-crafted with effort, in-depth research, and is composed of of natural and pet-safe ingredients including Beeswax, Shea butter, Coconut oil and Sunflower oil to protect those curious pets wanting to give it a lick.
